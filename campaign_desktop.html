About Campaign

SPORTS & EDUCATION FOR A BETTER FUTURE! Helping others to help themselves is our device! Everybody has a right to learn - but not everybody has the opportunity! Therefore it is our aim to support rural areas in India and give the children a chance for a better education and therefore for a better future. Besides education we believe in sports, as sports is important for a healthy body, for so many important skills in life like team spirit, motivation, going to our limits and achieving something. As our running project in Rajasthan proofed its success already, we are planning to set up more Hockey Villages all over India. Especially in areas where there is a huge percentage of child labor we are eager to implement our project to provide a real childhood to the children and a vision for their future. Besides quality education, we want to develope our own Hockey Village league so that children from all Hockey Villages can compete amongst each other and grow and improve their skills. On the astro turf (that we are hopefully able to lay out by end of 2015) in our second Hockey Village Jatwara is the home of our Hockey Village India Academy where we will recruit the best players from all Hockey Villages and provide them boarding facilities and international coaching standard. Our aim is it to find a cooperate partner who is employing our children later on. Sportspeople are the best and most reliable employees you can ever dream of – researches from abroad have proofed that.
